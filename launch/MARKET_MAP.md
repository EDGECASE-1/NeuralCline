## 🧬 The Agentic AI Maturity Curve

NeuralCline maps to the natural evolution of agentic AI adoption:

```
Stage 1: NOVICE (Today)
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────
  "Let me try this AI coding thing"
  → Crashes are accepted as normal
  → Session loss is expected
  → No production trust

Stage 2: DEPENDENT (6-12 months)
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────
  "I rely on this for daily work, but..."
  → Crashes are frustrating but tolerated
  → Workarounds exist (manual state saving)
  → Partial production trust

Stage 3: PRODUCTION (12-24 months)
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────
  "This is part of our critical workflow"
  → Crashes are unacceptable
  → Session safety is mandatory
  → Full production trust ← NeuralCline enables this

Stage 4: INFRASTRUCTURE (24-36 months)
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────
  "This is as essential as our database"
  → Session safety is built into the platform
  → Crashes are automatically recovered
  → Zero-downtime agent operations
```

**NeuralCline accelerates the transition from Stage 1 → Stage 3 by eliminating the most critical failure mode.**
